            <h1><a name="affection-levels" id="affection-levels">Character's Affection Levels</a></h1>
            <p>The villagers affection levels in <a href="http://www.amazon.com/mn/search/?_encoding=UTF8&tag=ultimoriga-20&linkCode=ur2&camp=1789&creative=390957&field-keywords=harvest%20moon%20back%20to%20nature&url=search-alias%3Daps" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Harvest Moon: Back to Nature</a> work much like the girls however the numbers are all much lower.</p>
            <p>All characters start out with 50 affection and can have a maximum of 255 affection in this videogame. There unfortunately isn't really any way to check their affection levels though. </p>
            <p>Every night there's a very low percent chance that a character's affection might randomly go down 1 point. As long as you try to talk to people at least somewhat often then this should never be a problem. </p>
            <p>As long as you speak to a character once or give them a gift their affection will go up by 1 at the start of the next day in the game.</p>
            <p>You can give multiple gifts to a character in the same day and increase your affection with them. You don't get quite as nice an amount of affection from multiple gifts as you do with the girls though.</p>
            <p>&nbsp;</p>

            <h1><a name="harvest-sprites" id="harvest-sprites">The Harvest Sprites</a></h1>
            <p>You can find the Harvest Sprites in the smal hut behind the church. You can ask the sprites to work on your farm doing things such as watering crops, taking care of your animals or harvesting crops. For the record I have never hired the Harvest Sprites to do any work in any of my playthroughs of this videogame.</p>
            <p>If you speak to them twice you can get them to work on your farm. During the spring all they talk about is a tea party and you'll have to speak to them 5 times to get them to do any work for you.</p>
            <p>The Harvest Sprites have heart levels that go up every 25 affection points. They also lose 2 affection every time you make them work.</p>
            <p>They also have a hidden skill level for various jobs that starts out at 0 for each job. As you get them to do work for you this skill level will go up by one point and they'll eventually get less terrible at doing the work. The maximum skill level for each job is 255 points.</p>
